Millets

​

  • Buy game cover mixtures at the best prices and with next day delivery
  • Provides warm, shelter and feed for game birds
  • Attract wild seed eating birds such as finches
  • White millet is particularly attractive to grey and red-legged partridges
  • When required to last longer into the season it performs well if sown with maize
